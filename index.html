Welcome to Satya Aircon

To solve your different cooling problems

Satya Aircon & Eng. Services Pvt. Ltd. is a company incorporated with a vision to provide state of the art, technically appropriate, professional solutions to corporates in the Eastern India and Chhatisgarh. Satya Aircon has more than 20 years experience in the field of heating, designing, installing and commissioning HVAC solutions for various sectors of the industry, like corporate offices, software development centers, hotels, hospitals & pharma labs, industrial & ventelation applications and the hospitality industry.
